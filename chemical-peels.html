Chemical peels

What it is

A reset for the surface

A chemical peel improves the appearance and texture of your skin. A specialised solution lifts away dead skin cells and stimulates healthy new cell growth.

As the old skin gently sheds, you will notice a fresher, clearer, more radiant complexion. Peel strength is chosen at your consult, and if you have never had one before we start gently and build from there.

Expect some shedding

Light flaking for three to five days is normal and a sign the peel is working. Do not pick or exfoliate it away.

Pause your actives

Stop retinol, AHAs and BHAs for five days before your appointment and for a week afterwards.

SPF every day

Fresh skin burns easily. Daily SPF 50 for at least two weeks after your peel protects the result you paid for.
