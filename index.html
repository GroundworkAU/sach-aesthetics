About the studio

Skin, treated properly

A small studio dedicated to high quality, tailored treatments that enhance natural beauty and support long term skin health.

Every service is built around your skin rather than a set menu. Chemical peels, microneedling and targeted facials are all customised at the time of your appointment, so what you leave with is the result of a proper consult and not a guess.

From me to you

Beauty is about more than how we look. It is about how we feel.

I have worked hard to create a space where self love, confidence and comfort come first. Every treatment is meant to be an act of self care, and you should leave feeling more confident in your own skin. We all deserve that.
