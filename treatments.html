Before you book

Let me know if you are pregnant, using prescription retinoids or roaccutane, or have had recent injectables. Some treatments need to be adjusted or rescheduled.

Patch testing

Lash and brow tinting requires a patch test at least 24 hours before your first appointment. Get in touch to arrange one.

Aftercare

You will leave with written aftercare for your treatment. Sun protection is non negotiable after peels and microneedling.
