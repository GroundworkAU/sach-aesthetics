Lashes & brows

The approach

Groomed, not redrawn

Lifts, tints and lamination that work with the lashes and brows you already have.

Shape is mapped to your face rather than a trend, and tint is matched to your natural colouring. A lash lift lasts six to eight weeks. Brow lamination holds for four to six.

First time having anything tinted? A patch test at least 24 hours before your appointment is required. Get in touch and we will sort it.
